From Our Side

What do we do?

Amid this pandemic, people are having a tough time finding hospitals and medical stores having masks and sanitizers. Also this is not safe for people to roam around city to find these essential services. Hence we provide a platform for people to find the hospitals and pharmacies which are working during the lockdown and equipped with essential services like masks,sanitizers or not. We update the information daily to ensure its usefulness among the community. In due time, we will be adding more features to facilitate people protect themselves from Corona.

How it works?

We collect data from pharmacies all over India regarding the availability of masks and sanitizers to facilitate the required ones. We ping the medical store owners daily to update the availability of masks, sanitizers.

Is this available in my city?

We are trying to contact pharmacies and hospitals all over India to expand our reach as soon as possible. In this campaign we would like you to contribute by refering this platform to your known pharmacy owners.
